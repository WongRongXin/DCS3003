HONOR OF KINGS

Heroes are the focus of Honor of Kings, clashing in multiplayer online battles of up to ten heroes (5v5).

They are divided into six classes, each with a distinct role on the battlefield and created with a lane in mind.Clever players will find ways to effectively use them in any conceivable scenario.















凯

A tank is a high HP hero whose primary role is to hold the line and absorb enemy attention and damage, allowing teammates to attack, flank, or withdraw as needed.







花木兰

Primary offensive fighters excelling in the Clash lane, leveling up quickly and potentially dominating the battle if employed correctly.







孔明

Ranged fighters with area of effect (AoE) skills and crowd control abilities, perfect for the Mid-Lane.







百里守约

High damage, low HP, the archetypal glass cannon. Very good for overcoming defenses and evaporating enemy HP, but require proper care and maintenance from a Support class.







橘右京

Masters of the Jungle, roaming the map targeting mobs to gain gold for the team and level up experience points. They have excellent explosive damage and mobility, making them good for flanking attacks.







大乔

The healer class of hero, focusing on healing, protection, and some crowd control. While might not be seen as glamorous, a good healer can sustain an attack and turn the tide of a seemingly unwinnable clash.
